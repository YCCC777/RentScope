RentScope

Is your rent fair? See HUD Fair Market Rents on Zillow and Apartments.com.
Know instantly if a listing is overpriced — and whether Section 8 vouchers apply.

What RentScope does

📊HUD Fair Market Rents overlay on Zillow & Apartments.com
🏠Studio through 4BR FMR table per listing
📍ZIP-level precision (38,000+ ZIPs)
🎫Section 8 HCV payment standard check
🏢Multi-unit buildings: per floor plan breakdown with individual diff%
⚠️Market-relative fraud signal
🔍Manual ZIP code lookup for any US ZIP

How to use

  1. Go to zillow.com or apartments.com and open a rental listing
  2. RentScope automatically shows an FMR overlay on the listing page
  3. The overlay shows the full FMR table (Studio–4BR) — your unit size is highlighted
  4. See the % above or below FMR at a glance — green means fair, red means overpriced
  5. On multi-unit buildings, see a per floor plan breakdown (Studio / 1BR / 2BR …) with individual diff% for each unit type
  6. Check Section 8 HCV eligibility — whether the listing is within payment standard limits
  7. Use the popup's ZIP lookup to check any area's FMR without opening a listing

Data sources

🏛️ HUD Fair Market Rents (FY2025)

Source: U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development. Fair Market Rents (FMRs) represent HUD's estimate of the 40th percentile gross rent for standard quality units in each area. RentScope uses SAFMR (Small Area FMRs) for ZIP-level precision where available, with county-level FMRs as fallback for the rest of the country.

38,642 ZIP codes (SAFMR) + 2,600+ counties · updated annually in October when HUD releases new fiscal year data

🗺️ Census ZCTA–County Crosswalk

Source: U.S. Census Bureau — ZCTA to county relationship file. Used to map ZIP codes to county FIPS codes for the county-level FMR fallback. County assignment uses land area overlap as the tiebreaker for multi-county ZIPs.

33,000+ ZIP-to-county mappings · Census 2020

All data is from public U.S. government sources. RentScope does not collect any personal information and does not upload anything to any server.

For informational purposes only. FMR represents HUD's area-wide estimate — individual landlords are not required to comply. Section 8 HCV eligibility depends on local PHA payment standards, which may differ from the HUD cap shown here.
